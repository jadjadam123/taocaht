About TaoChat

TaoChat is described as an avant-garde communication protocol that goes beyond conventional messaging services. It harnesses the power of blockchain technology to introduce a platform that offers encrypted text messages, secure voice calls, on-chain message and image storage, and efficient group chat capabilities.

TaoChat aims to provide sophisticated communication and ironclad security, combining Web3 innovation with privacy protection.
